Our Heritage
1999
Founded

A Legacy Rooted
in the Land

The Merabet family has cultivated a profound relationship with Algerian soil for over two decades. What began as a local agricultural endeavor has evolved into a structured consulting enterprise with international reach — without ever losing the values of the earth.

1999
The Foundation
The Merabet family establishes its first agricultural operation in Algeria, focusing on sustainable local cultivation practices.
2008
Regional Expansion
Operations expand to three provinces, integrating modern irrigation and crop-yield consulting for partner farms across the region.
2015
International Reach
ACM establishes partnerships with European and African importers, bringing Algerian agricultural quality to global markets.
Today
ACM — The Consulting Era
A fully integrated agronomy and consulting firm, offering end-to-end agricultural advisory services alongside premium goods trading.
